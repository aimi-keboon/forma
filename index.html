About FORMA

FORMA is a platform built around professional credibility. Every trainer is verified, ensuring that clients connect only with trusted professionals. It’s not about mass signups — it’s about building a community where reputation matters.

Our goal is to strengthen the fitness industry through accountability, transparency, and verified skillsets — allowing professionals to grow without compromising standards.

The Value We Bring

  • For Trainers: Access to clients who prioritize professionalism and results.
  • For Clients: A curated list of certified trainers vetted for experience and credibility.
  • For the Industry: Raising the baseline for trust, safety, and professionalism in fitness training.

How It Works

  1. Apply – Submit your information to be part of the early network.
  2. Verify – Trainers go through document and experience verification.
  3. Connect – Verified professionals appear in the FORMA directory for client discovery.
