Core Concept

Verified, Credible Network

Only certified and vetted professionals can join. This raises the baseline of trust for clients and protects the value of trainer expertise.

Simple, Premium Marketplace

Clients discover trainers by specialty, location, and rating; trainers set availability and deliver sessions at premium rates.

Revenue Streams

Verification Fee

One-time onboarding fee.

RM50 per trainer to cover document and identity verification.

Session Commission

Take-rate on bookings.

10–15% per session (e.g., RM15–22.50 on RM150/session).

Premium Subscriptions

Visibility & tools.

Pro / Elite tiers: featured listings, analytics, marketing boosts, optional insurance add-ons.

Corporate Wellness

B2B programs.

Contracted access to verified trainers for companies and communities; pricing is project-based.

Value Proposition

For Trainers

Verified status, premium clientele, transparent pricing (e.g., RM150/session), flexible bookings, and upcoming insurance coverage.

For Clients

Curated, verified professionals; search by specialty/location; secure payment; consistent, results-driven training.

Growth Strategy

Phase 1 – Network Building

Early access program to onboard verified trainers; establish brand around credibility and standards.

Phase 2 – Client Acquisition

Launch client-facing flows; emphasize "Verified Trainers Only" positioning and city-specific campaigns.

Phase 3 – Regional Expansion

Scale through SEA; partner with local cert bodies for streamlined verification.

Phase 4 – Data & Analytics

Introduce dashboards, training outcomes, and corporate wellness reporting.
